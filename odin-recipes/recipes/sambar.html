Paneer Biryani Recipe

Description
Paneer biryani is a mildly spiced and delicately flavored dum cooked, traditional biryani recipe. Simply moreish and flavorful paneer biryani that pairs beautifully with any raita. A dum cooked biryani takes time to make but the results are always awesome. You can make this tasty biryani on a stove-top or in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Basmati rice thoroughly rinsed and drained
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger and garlic
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • Â½ teaspoon garam masala,chilli powder,cumin powder
  • 400 gms paneer cut into cubes
  • 1 cup fried onions
  • 3 tablespoon tomato paste
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the rice with the spices and salt till all the water evaporates and the rice is about halfway done. Spread it out on a large platter so that the grains do not clump and set aside.
  2. Add in paneer and tomatoes and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes till the paneer is nicely coated with all the spices. Do not cook the tomatoes for too long since we want them to retain their shape and not give out too much moisture. This should be fairly thick and the paneer pieces should be well coated with the spice paste.
  3. Spread about half the paneer mix at the bottom of the pot. Then layer over with half of the pre-cooked rice, and top that with half the amounts of the cilantro, mint and fried onions.
  4. Sprinkle the rose water on top, and cook covered on low heat for 10-15 minutes till the rice is completely cooked through. Serve warm with a side of Raita.
